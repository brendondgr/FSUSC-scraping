Section: Field Definition - Morphometrics
Morphometrics is the field of multivariate statistical analysis concerned with the quantification of shape, the description of shape variability, the assessment of group differences in shape, and the covariation of shape with other variables.
<---->
Section: FSU Morphlab Activities
The FSU Morphlab is involved in all aspects of morphometric research, including:
- The development of new methods
- The creation of software
- The novel application of morphometic methods to a diverse array of research problems.
